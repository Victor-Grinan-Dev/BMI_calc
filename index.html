BMI

Make a program that calculates body mass index. The BMI is defined as the body mass divided by the square of the body height, and is universally expressed in units of kg/m2, resulting from mass in kilograms and height in metres. Based on result display correct range: BMI Status 18.5 or less Underweight 18.5 to 24.9 Normal or Healthy Weight 25.0 to 29.9 Pre-obesity 30.0 or more Obesity
